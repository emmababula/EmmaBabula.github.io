About Me

My name is Emma Babula, and I am an astronomer and aspiring data analyst with a background in astrophysics research and science communication. I specialize in analyzing telescope data, generating lightcurves, and conducting photometric studies on Centaurs and other small bodies in the Solar System.

I’m passionate about combining technical tools such as SQL, Python, Tableau, and Power BI to uncover meaningful insights from complex datasets. I’ve presented my work at the 245th American Astronomical Society meeting and the 56th Lunar and Planetary Science Conference, and I’m constantly seeking new opportunities to grow and apply my skills across research and industry projects.
